Bass Trainer

A personal bass practice app with timed sessions, interactive exercises, and progress tracking — built for daily practice.

  • Structured practice sessions with configurable block durations
  • Fretboard mastery quiz — guess the note on any string and fret
  • Ear training — identify intervals by ear (simple, diatonic, or all)
  • Rhythm and improvisation exercises with backing tracks
  • Practice stats — track time spent per week, month, and year
  • Sign in with Google or create an account to save your progress
