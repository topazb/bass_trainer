Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we store your Google account ID, email address, and display name to identify your account. When you sign in with a username and password, we store your username and a hashed (bcrypt) password. Guest sessions store no personal information.

Practice data

We store your practice session history (duration, blocks completed, quiz scores) to show you personal statistics. This data is linked to your account and stored on our server.

What we do NOT do

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not track you across other websites.

Data deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at topazb@gmail.com.
